About JDS Document Centre

Built on passion, quality, and a commitment to serve, discover how we got started.

Founded in 2024 by Joan Johnson-Brown, Shanique Wisdom, and Dain Thorpe, JDS Document Centre began with a mission to bring fast, affordable, and professional printing services to the community.

What started in a small garage with one machine and big dreams has grown into a trusted name in document solutions across Jamaica. We serve individuals, schools, and businesses with everything from quick prints to large-scale projects.

At the core of our journey is a commitment to quality, service, and simplicity, all delivered with heart and hustle.
